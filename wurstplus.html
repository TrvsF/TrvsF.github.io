Wurst Plus a trio of minecraft mods that would aid in a subset of servers featuring 'crystal pvp'. each was written in java, using gradle as a buildtool & mixins to inject code at runtime, by myself.

wurst plus 3
the final instalment of the trilogy, made alongside madmeg & a bunch of other contributers that you can find on the github. this version was the first to be (almost) always full open source & therefore featured lots of realse trouble due to my inexperience. in the end, however, this version was the one where i understood the most what i was doing, though i was getting sick of minecraft at this point.
(java)


wurst plus 2
every 'good' minecraft mod needs a good sequel. this realse featured some cool ideas, a pasted gui, & lots of controversy. this was probably the 'best' instalment, mostly due to the speed i was working, the fun i was having, & fairly fast pace the meta was shifting.
(java)
